Guidelines > Best Practice

Best Practice

Follow these guidelines to ensure consistent and effective use of the design system.

Design Principles

Consistency

Use components and patterns consistently across all interfaces to create a cohesive user experience.

Simplicity

Keep interfaces simple and focused. Remove unnecessary complexity and cognitive load.

Feedback

Always provide clear feedback for user actions to confirm that the system is responding.

Flexibility

Design for different contexts and user needs while maintaining consistency.
