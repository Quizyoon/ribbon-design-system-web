Colors

The color system provides a structured approach to using colors consistently across the LINE GIFTSHOP platform. It defines how colors are organized, named, and applied throughout the design system.

Background Foreground Border

Role-based Color System

Systematic Color Structure

Semantic color tokens define colors based on the role and purpose of UI elements. Each category such as Background, Foreground, and Border provides colors appropriate for the function of the element. This system ensures consistent color usage by designers and developers, and automatically applies appropriate colors when switching themes, including dark mode.

Background & Surface

Background tokens define the background color of UI surfaces such as pages, cards, and modals. They express hierarchy through Primary, Secondary, and Tertiary levels, and convey meaning through Brand, Accent, and Status colors.

Foreground & Text

Foreground tokens define the colors of foreground elements such as text and icons. They provide highly readable colors that consider background contrast, and ensure accessibility with On-color tokens that match each background color.

Border & Divider

Border tokens define colors used for borders, dividers, and outlines. They clarify boundaries between elements and provide visual distinction based on state or importance.
